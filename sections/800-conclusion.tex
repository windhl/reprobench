\section{Conclusion}
% 总结
\sys{} evaluates whether LLM agents can move from a CVE identifier to auditable evidence of IoT firmware vulnerability reproduction. Our evaluation results present the strong performance through information gathering and firmware analysis, but a collapse at rehosting (2.7/20). More than half of runs substitute simulated reproductions, and real-target success is achievable only on same-architecture devices. Future cyber agents can be evaluated not only on plausible reports, but on whether their actions remain grounded in the real artifacts.
