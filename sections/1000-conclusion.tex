\section{Conclusion}
% 总结
\sys{} evaluates whether LLM agents can move from a CVE identifier to auditable evidence of IoT firmware vulnerability reproduction. Across 450 runs under best-of-three scoring, agents average 50.1/100, with strong performance through information gathering and firmware analysis, but collapse at rehosting (2.7/20). 69.8\% of runs substitute simulated reproductions; real-target success is achievable only on same-architecture devices where \texttt{chroot} bypasses emulation. Future cyber agents must be evaluated not only on plausible reports, but on whether their actions remain grounded in the real artifacts they claim to reproduce.
